About Us

CRAST is all about pushing the limits of applied science to solve society's toughest challenges through top-notch research and interdisciplinary collaboration.

At the CRAST, we are committed to advancing the frontiers of scientific knowledge and technological innovation. Through interdisciplinary collaboration, and a dedication to excellence, we strive to address the most pressing challenges facing society today.

Mission

To advance scientific knowledge and technological innovation through nterdisciplinary research and collaboration, addressing real-world challenges and contributing to societal development.

Vision:

To be a leading research center recognized globally for excellence in applied science and technology, fostering sustainable development and innovative solutio

Core Values

Integrity, innovation, and impact guide our research and operations, ensuring ethical practices, creative solutions, and meaningful contributions to society

Facilities and Infrastructure

Our state-of-the-art facilities provide researchers with the tools and resources they need to conduct groundbreaking research. From advanced laboratories and high-performance computing clusters to specialized equipment and instrumentation, we offer a supportive environment where innovation thrives.

Collaboration and Partnerships

Collaborative ethos: CRST prioritizes partnerships with industry, government, academia, and nonprofits.

Cutting-edge resources: Our facilities boast advanced labs, computing clusters, and specialized equipment.

Interdisciplinary innovation: We foster collaboration across fields, driving transformative research.

Streams

Our research spans a wide range of disciplines within science and technology, including:

Architecture

The art and science of designing and constructing buildings and other physical structures.

Civil Engineering

Engineering discipline focused on infrastructure development like roads, bridges, and dams.

Civil and Rural Engineering

A subset of civil engineering focusing on infrastructure development in rural areas.

Computer Engineering

Combines computer science and electrical engineering to design and develop computer systems.

Electrical and Electronics Engineering

Focuses on the study and application of electricity, electronics, and electromagnetism.

Electronics and Communication Engineering

Deals with electronic devices, circuits, and communication systems like telecommunication.
